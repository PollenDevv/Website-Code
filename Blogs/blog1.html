How to get ideas for projects

We've all been there, when you have the motivation to do something but you don't know what to do. So this begs the question, what should you do?

I generally think there are three different ways to approach this problem

  1. Use an online generator
  2. Use a special database
  3. Use an A.I. chatbot
I will go through each one and describe.

For the online generator it generally has to be a specific problem that multiple people have, like a character name generator or a game jam theme generator.

Now for the special database it is pretty much the same as the first one but instead of a random number generator that would pick one of the items in the database. Again it is a very specific problem with a weird solution.

The most general but also the most unreliable choice would be the A.I chatbot which is pretty much an online generator but the generator is just making stuff up as you go.

This was something that I wrote about because I didn't know what to write about lol
To sum it all up, the best ways to come up with ideas are
  1. Use an online generator
  2. Use a special database
  3. Use an A.I. chatbot
or you could just make it up on your own ideas lol
