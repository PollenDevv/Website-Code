How to get out of tutorial purgatory abyss

When starting programming it's easy to think that programming is just about typing words on a screen, but when in reality that is only a small part, the main part of programming is problem solving and design choices.

So when you start programming it can be very overwhelming, so what do you do? You look up a tutorial or follow a guidebook, this is fine but the problem comes when you keep using tutorials, again and again. At what point are you following a tutorial instead of programming yourself, it also takes away trying to complete a solo project by yourself.

The easiest ways out ot very simple: stop using tutorials, unless its new and you have to watch a tutorial, or you don't know the syntax, you generally don't want to use a tutorial. But you may be stuck trying to code. There may be a couple of reasons

  • Don't have an idea of what to do, in that case try my first blog
  • Don't know how to go about it. what you want to do is break it into smaller pieces, I will have a blog on that soon
  • Don't know the syntax, in this case you kind of go against what I've been saying but just for syntax, you can look up a tutorial as I mention earlier in the blog
